Reference guide for dynamic variables

Dynamic Variables

Welcome Function

  • {mention} - Mentions the member who joins
  • {server} - The server's name
  • {user} - The username of the member who joins
  • {count} - The total number of server members after the event
  • {count-th} - The total number of server members (with suffixes) after the event

Vanity Roles

  • {mention} - Mentions the member adding your invite link
  • {server} - The server's name
  • {user} - The username of the member adding your invite link
  • {count} - The total number of members with the exclusive role
  • {count-th} - The total number of members with the exclusive role (with suffixes)
  • {role} - Mentions the exclusive role
  • {rolename} - The exclusive role's name
  • {link} - The server's vanity invite link
