Discord Server Rules

1. No Toxic Attitudes

Avoid behaviors, language, and attitudes that are harmful, negative, or disruptive to others. This includes bullying, trolling, harassment, hate speech, discrimination, and any other behavior that creates a negative atmosphere in the community or makes other members uncomfortable.

2. No Offensive, Sensitive or NSFW Content

Refrain from posting any content containing hate speech, discriminatory remarks, offensive language, or sensitive material that could trigger or distress some members. This includes but is not limited to, content related to racism, sexism, homophobia, politics, religion, violence, and other forms of discrimination or sensitive content.

This server is a mostly SFW community. Swearing and sexual-related discussions are not allowed in any channels, including excessive gore/extreme violence, self-harm or harming others, pornography, and excessive sexual content.

Messages that involve aggressive shipping of characters or are overly suggestive are not allowed. Furthermore, obsessive admiration or fawning over other members, especially based on gender, is not tolerated. Please also refrain from sending any messages that may be deemed as venting, ranting, or related to roleplaying.

AutoMod is implemented to block instances of swearing and other inappropriate language. Here are an incomplete list of banned words, which means that words not included in the list might still be flagged. Please do not attempt to bypass these AutoMod rules, as doing so will result in immediate disciplinary actions.

3. No Harassment or Sharing of Personal Information

Any behaviors that could be interpreted as intimidating, threatening, targeting, or insulting are prohibited. This includes actions such as stalking, doxing, blackmailing, impersonating, and any other form of harassment.

Refrain from sharing any personal or sensitive information on the server without the explicit consent of all parties involved, including but not limited to names, addresses, phone numbers, email addresses, passwords, or any other identifying information that could compromise a member's safety, security, or reputation.

Please do not share screenshots or snippets of your or other people’s DM with other members if you are not explicitly permitted to do so.

4. Be Respectful to Everyone

Treat all members of the community with kindness and consideration. Avoid using offensive language or making derogatory comments, as well as refrain from engaging in any form of bullying or harassment.

Members should also especially be respectful to server staff by following their instructions. If a member has an issue with another member or staff, they should raise their concerns toward higher-positioned staff members or owners in an appropriate and respectful manner.

5. No Incitement

Refrain from promoting rule-breaking behavior, inciting others to engage in blatantly offensive actions, or encouraging conflicts between members.

6. No Spamming

Any behaviors that involve sending repetitive, disruptive, or excessive messages, mentions, attachments, links, emojis, or tags are not tolerated. “Ghost-pinging” is also prohibited.

7. No Self-advertising or Soliciting

Posting in any channels or direct messaging any members of this server to promote services, products, or social media is strictly forbidden. All advertisements must go through our owners before they can be posted.

Do not post any harmful, phishing, or suspicious links as well.

8. Be Mindful of Channel Usage

Stay on topic in order to keep conversations focused and productive. Please keep all conversations in English unless otherwise specified.

Please use channels for their specific purpose. Check any pinned messages, sticky messages, and channel topics before posting any content. Do not use excessive bot commands in chat channels (usage once/twice is exceptional).

9. Respect Copyright of Others

Give credit when sharing content that does not belong to you. Content or reposts in the server not authorized by the original creator will be removed.

You may not scrap or use any content posted in this server by any means without giving credit to our server or the respective creators.

10. Follow Terms of Service

Hacking, modding, cheating discussions, account trading, or any other behaviors that may violate the Genshin Impact ToS, except leaks discussion, which can only be done in #⁠✧・genshin-leaks. Any member found engaging in such activities will face an instant ban from the Discord server.

Members must also follow the Discord ToS. Violations, such as bypassing the age limit of using Discord, sharing explicit content of minors, glorifying self-harm or suicide, and sharing pirated or illegally acquired content, may also result in an instant ban from the server.

Remarks

Upon becoming a member of this server, it is expected that you have read and agreed to abide by the established rules.

The staff members of this Discord server have been granted the power to enforce the rules and impose disciplinary measures at their discretion. The ultimate decision-making authority lies with the owners and should not be challenged.

If any member has concerns about staff members abusing their power or making questionable decisions, or wishes to appeal a prior disciplinary action, they can reach out to one of the owners with a comprehensive report of the situation. Staff members' actions must not be debated publicly nor discussed with other server members that are not directly involved with corresponding incidents.

It is important to note that every punishment will be evaluated on a case-by-case basis, and the rules apply not just within the server but also to your DM with other server members as well as other Discord servers.

These rules are simply in place to ensure the well-being of the community and create a welcoming and secure space for all members. Other than that, enjoy your stay in Genshin Impact Cafe!
